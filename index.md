# Norwood Open Mic

![Norwood Open Mic Gravatar](https://www.gravatar.com/avatar/adcda19cd81818cf19d8e45caa96682b)

SideDoor Cafe - September 20th 2026

The Norwood Open Mic (NOM) will be an opportunity for people to share a personal story, original poem, original song or cover song in a radically supportive environment.  

The goal of this open mic is to create connection among the people present at the open mic. 

If you have a moment please fill out this survey to let me know if you are interested in this event.
